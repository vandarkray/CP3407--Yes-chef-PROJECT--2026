## Actual Velocity Calculation

Estimated user-story days after planning poker:

E1 = **21 days**

Actual working days used in iteration-1:

A1 = **15 days**

Formula provided in the practical:

E1 / v1 = A1

Substituting values:

21 / v1 = 15

v1 ≈ **1.4**

The **actual velocity is approximately 1.4**.

This means the team completed the planned work **faster than originally estimated**.

## Plan for Iteration 2

Based on the velocity calculated from Iteration 1, our team will adjust the backlog and planning for **Iteration 2**.

The next iteration will focus on:

- Backend development

This will allow the system to move from a front-end prototype to a **fully functional integrated application**.
